## Working Papers

**Predicting Idiosyncratic Price Jumps with Embeddings**
<br>Adrian Enders
<br>*Working Paper*, 2025
<br>[[PDF]](/files/Adrian_Enders_CV.pdf) <!-- replace with paper link when available -->

We develop a framework for predicting idiosyncratic price jumps in equity markets using text and return embeddings. Standard asset pricing models struggle to forecast jump events due to their rare and non-linear nature. We address this by learning low-dimensional representations of firm-level information and training classifiers on these embeddings to produce out-of-sample jump probability forecasts. We evaluate performance using standard discrimination metrics and find significant predictability relative to factor-model benchmarks.
