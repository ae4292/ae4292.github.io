I am an incoming PhD student in Finance at [London Business School](https://www.london.edu/), joining in Fall 2026. I am currently an Economist Apprentice at Amazon, where I work closely with Amazon Scholars on internal research in forecasting, portfolio construction, and time series econometrics. My work applies econometric models including Double Machine Learning and Bayesian Structural Time Series to business problems at scale.

My research interests span **asset pricing**, **deep learning for finance**, **causal inference**, and **corporate finance**. I am particularly interested in predicting idiosyncratic price jumps using embedding-based representations of firm-level information.

## Research Interests

- **Asset Pricing** — idiosyncratic price jump prediction, factor models, return predictability
- **Deep Learning for Finance** — generative models for financial time series, representation learning
- **Causal Inference** — treatment effect estimation, outlier-robust methods, observational studies
- **Corporate Finance** — empirical corporate finance, firm behavior, capital structure
